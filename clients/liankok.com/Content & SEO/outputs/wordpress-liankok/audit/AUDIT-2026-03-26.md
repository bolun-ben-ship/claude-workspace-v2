## Executive Summary

Lian Kok Electrical's website has **significant foundational issues** that are actively suppressing its Google visibility. The site has strong brand credentials (ABB authorised distributor since 1991, established B2B presence) but the WordPress installation is heavily polluted with leftover theme demo content, has no performance caching, and is missing several high-value structured data signals.

The good news: most of the highest-impact fixes are **settings changes** (Yoast, WordPress admin) that require no development work. The site can realistically move from ~38 to 65+ within 2–3 weeks of focused remediation.

### Top 5 Critical Issues
1. **~30 automotive/theme demo pages** fully indexed and in sitemap — actively diluting topical authority
2. **No caching plugin** — TTFB at 1,071ms, estimated mobile LCP 6–9s (Google threshold: 2.5s)
3. **46 H1 tags on homepage, 28 on /products/** — Google cannot determine page topic
4. **`/shop/` page renders empty** — zero products in DOM, crawlable dead-end
5. **Dual sitemap plugins active simultaneously** — Google XML Sitemaps + Yoast both running

### Top 5 Quick Wins (under 30 minutes each)
1. Fix `WebSite.name` in Yoast (currently set to URL instead of brand name) — 2 minutes
2. Noindex cart/checkout pages via Yoast — 5 minutes
3. Add meta descriptions to `/products/` and `/shop/` — 10 minutes
4. Remove invalid sitemap entries from robots.txt — 10 minutes
5. Set a 1200×630px OG image in Yoast social settings — 10 minutes
