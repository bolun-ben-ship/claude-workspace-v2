## 8. Recommendations for Next Steps

   The deadline was 2026-03-27 and has now passed. Every product page has suppressed Product rich results in Google. This is a single theme edit taking ~15 minutes. Until this is fixed, all 10 product pages lose their star ratings and price snippets in SERPs.

   6 static pages — including Custom Mattress (630 impressions) and Showroom (400 impressions) — cannot receive SEO improvements until this is done. It's a 5-minute change in Shopline admin.

## 9. Engagement Summary

Owllight Sleep entered this engagement with an SEO Health Score of 37/100 — significantly below average for a DTC e-commerce brand. The core problem was not one large technical failure but the accumulated weight of many small, fixable issues: every product and blog page was showing Google the same store-default meta tags, the homepage had no H1 tag, all blog post schema was broken, and a 7-day priceValidUntil deadline was about to kill product rich results across the entire site.

In the first three days of the engagement, RightClick:AI executed the highest-impact API-available changes: all 10 active products and all 7 published blog posts now have individual, keyword-targeted SEO titles and meta descriptions for the first time. An additional 8 new blog posts — targeting Emma vs Owllight, Origin vs Owllight, mattress firmness guidance, cooling topper selection, and morning back pain — have been written and pushed as drafts, expanding the content library from 7 live posts toward the 3-month target of 13+.

The organic performance data shows early positive signals: organic search sessions are up 7.3% (150 → 161) and average GSC position has improved slightly (11.8 → 11.3) in the first 3 days. The meta tag changes will take 2–4 weeks to flow through Google's re-indexing cycle; the April 20 GSC pull will be the first meaningful performance test.

The site's biggest remaining opportunities — theme-level technical fixes, static page SEO access (requires token scope upgrade), and the `priceValidUntil` expiry — all require client-side action or developer access that falls outside API execution scope. These are documented clearly above and should be actioned immediately to prevent further ranking suppression.

The 3-month target of 60/100 health score and +40% organic sessions remains achievable, provided the theme fixes and token scope upgrade are completed in April and the draft blog posts are published promptly.
